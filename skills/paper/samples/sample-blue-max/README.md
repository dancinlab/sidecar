Minimal BLUE-MAX-only structure. Every claim in the body resolves to an `atom:<id>` whose `hexa verify` verdict is 🔵 SUPPORTED-FORMAL (closed-form algebraic identity). No 🟢 numerical, no 🟠 orange, no citation-fallback.

Copy via:

```
/paper sample sample-blue-max <your-slug>
```

Then replace the audit-table rows with your domain's claims and atom ids. Anything that only achieves 🟢 SUPPORTED-NUMERICAL goes into a sibling 🟢 paper that cites the atoms registered here.
